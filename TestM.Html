What is Machining?

Machining means shaping metal by cutting bits away—like carving wood, but with machines!

Main Processes

  • Turning: Creates round parts like wheels using a lathe.
  • Milling: Removes material from flat or angled parts.
  • Drilling: Makes holes with a spinning tool.
  • Grinding: Smooths metal surfaces with an abrasive wheel.
